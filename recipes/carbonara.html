Carbonara

Description

A traditional Italian pasta dish

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale

Steps

  1. Salt water for Pasta
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Cook Guanciale in pan
  4. Combine Pecorino Romano with Eggs and Pepper
  5. Put Pasta in pan with Guanciale
  6. Pour Egg slurry into pan
  7. Add Pasta water if necessary
  8. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
